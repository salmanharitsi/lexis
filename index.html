How Lexis Works

Here's how our smart system works to help you!

User Login and Function Selection

Users log into Lexis via web or mobile. They then select a specific function, such as legal questions or document analysis.

Input and Tokenization

Users submit their query or upload documents. The text is tokenized for precise processing. Note, for now you only can input pdf files.

RAG and LLM Processing

Lexis uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to retrieve relevant legal data. The Falcon LLM then generates a detailed and accurate response.

Response Delivery

The response is delivered in the chosen format (text, PDF, etc.). Tokens are consumed based on query complexity.

Feedback and Continuous Learning

Users provide feedback on the response quality, which helps Lexis improve and refine future interactions. You can also continue this conversation next time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here, we address common queries to help you better understand our AI-powered legal assistant. Whether you're curious about our plans, how Lexis works, or data security, we've got you covered. Explore the questions below for quick answers to help you get the most out of Lexis.

1. What is Lexis?

Lexis is an AI-powered legal assistant that helps users understand legal concepts, analyze documents, and navigate legal processes. It utilizes advanced technologies like Falcon LLM and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide accurate and comprehensive legal information.

2. What can I do with the Free Plan?

The Free Plan offers basic access to general legal information, limited document analysis, and community support. It is suitable for simple queries and preliminary insights but has limited functionality compared to the paid plans.

3. How does the Pay-As-You-Go plan work?

The Pay-As-You-Go plan allows you to pay for detailed analysis of a specific legal issue. Once a case is initiated, it remains focused on the defined topic. If you need assistance with a different legal issue, you will need to start a new case and pay for that separately.

4. What does the Monthly Sultan Plan include?

The Monthly Sultan Plan offers unlimited access to all Lexis features, including advanced legal tools, unlimited queries, multi-language support, and priority customer service. This plan is ideal for frequent users who require comprehensive legal assistance.

5. Is Lexis available in multiple languages?

Currently, Lexis supports English and Indonesian. We are working on expanding our language and jurisdictional support to include more languages and countries soon.

6. How does Lexis ensure the accuracy of its responses?

Lexis utilizes high-quality datasets and advanced AI models to ensure accurate and reliable responses. Our team continuously updates the system with the latest legal information and user feedback to improve accuracy.

7. Is my data safe with Lexis?

Yes, we prioritize data privacy and security. Lexis follows strict data protection standards and complies with relevant regulations to safeguard user information.

8. Can I change my plan after subscribing?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Please contact our customer support for assistance with plan changes.

9. How do I provide feedback on Lexis?

We welcome user feedback to improve our services. You can rate responses and provide comments directly within the platform. Alternatively, you can contact our customer support team.

10. What if I encounter an issue or need help?

If you experience any issues or require assistance, please contact our customer support team. We offer various support channels, including email, chat, and a dedicated help center.

11. How does Lexis support the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)?

Lexis is committed to supporting SDG 10 (Reduced Inequality) and SDG 16 (Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions). We strive to make legal information accessible to all, reducing inequalities in legal access. Our tools promote transparency, accountability, and fair legal practices, contributing to the development of strong and just institutions.

12. How does Lexis contribute to reducing inequality?

By providing free and affordable access to legal information and resources, Lexis helps bridge the gap between those with limited access to legal services and those who can afford them. This commitment to accessibility supports our goal of reducing inequalities in legal representation and access to justice.
